# ShaktiHub App

## Product Case Study

ShaktiHub App is a commercial field enablement and retailer marketplace platform developed to improve Unilever’s route-to-market operations, product visibility, order management, payment processing, and sales performance tracking.

## Problem

Unilever’s field operations faced fragmentation across product availability, pricing visibility, retailer ordering, sales reporting, and reconciliation. Retailers had limited access to real-time product information, while sales officers relied heavily on manual reporting and slow operational feedback loops.

## Product Solution

I led the end-to-end development and launch of a platform that consolidated product data, enabled real-time stock and pricing visibility, supported order placement and payment workflows, and delivered operational dashboards for field teams and sales managers.

The platform was designed as a scalable, mobile-first solution for retailer onboarding and field sales enablement.

## My Role

As Product Lead, I coordinated the development process from product definition to launch. I supported the integration of product and inventory data into a unified system, implemented role-based access for 190 sales officers, designed mobile-first onboarding flows for retailers, and led training and change-management activities to accelerate adoption.

## Key Product Features

* Retailer onboarding
* Mobile-first user experience
* Unified product database
* Real-time stock visibility
* Pricing visibility
* Order placement
* Payment processing
* Role-based access control
* Sales officer dashboards
* Inventory and sales reporting
* Field team performance visibility

## Product Impact

The platform onboarded 13,000+ retailers and 190 sales officers and facilitates over $7 million in annual sales through the channel.

It improved order accuracy and speed, reduced manual reconciliation tasks, increased field productivity, strengthened retailer engagement, and provided Unilever with a reliable, auditable digital channel for sustained sales growth.
