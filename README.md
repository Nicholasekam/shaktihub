# ShaktiHub

ShaktiHub is a digital platform designed to support women-led distribution, field sales visibility, product access, and marketplace coordination.

## Project Overview

ShaktiHub strengthens last-mile distribution by enabling women entrepreneurs, retailers, and field teams to access product information, track sales activity, and improve route-to-market performance.

## Website

https://www.shaktihub.org/

## Key Focus Areas

- Women economic empowerment
- Last-mile distribution
- Retailer and reseller coordination
- Digital marketplace visibility
- Sales and field performance tracking

## My Role

I contributed to the project through strategy, product thinking, stakeholder coordination, field insights, and business development support.

## Project Value

ShaktiHub helps improve access to consumer goods, strengthens women-led enterprise participation, and supports data-driven decision-making across distribution networks.
